>> Hello! I'm Reed, and I'm pursuing a career transition into AI Safety. I come from a background in operations and systems building, having been the first employee at After The Tone—a wedding industry startup that I helped scale from the ground up to 50+ employees and over $20 million in revenue as Head of Operations.

>> My experience taught me how to build infrastructure in ambiguous situations, coordinate cross-functional teams, and make high-stakes decisions under pressure. I led a company-wide $1M+ software migration, designed customer support workflows that earned 3,000+ five-star reviews, and created the operational systems that enabled sustainable growth.

>> About five years ago, I read Nick Bostrom's Superintelligence, and it fundamentally changed my perspective. The potential trajectories for AI—both positive and catastrophic—made it clear that this is the most important challenge of our time. Recently, I've been consuming everything I can: The Alignment Problem, AI 2027, and re-reading Superintelligence.

>> I believe the technical problems of AI alignment are solvable with enough time and good faith cooperation. But the cooperation part—getting the right people working on this, building effective organizations, and coordinating efforts—might be even harder. That's where I think I can contribute.

>> Earlier this year, I completed a 7-day silent meditation retreat, which taught me a lot about presence, truth-seeking, and facing reality. It made me think about what kind of AI we might create if we approached it with that same mindful intention, rather than the speed and reactivity that currently dominates development. I want to bring that thoughtfulness into my work in AI Safety.

>> I'm currently exploring non-technical ways to help ensure that as many good, thoughtful people as possible are influencing the future of AI. Organizations like BlueDot Impact give me hope. I'm here to learn, contribute, and find where I can be most useful.
